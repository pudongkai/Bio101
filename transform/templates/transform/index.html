Usage

Now, we will introduce the workflows of our software briefly. The software is simple, such as putting and getting something from the fridge. The software is divided into two parts: 1. make the files compression, then convert them to DNA sequence; 2. transform DNA sequence inverse to form a file.
1) Encode There are two buttons on home page. It will jump to the CORE or DECODE show interface in the situation that users click the corresponding button. Meanwhile, menu bar can also complete the same task. If you have been familiar with our software, let's start! What you need to do is to click the choose   button, then select a file. Don't forget to click the submit  button. During the transforming time, you can drink a cup of coffee or scan NFL news. There is a pop-up reminder when the task is completed. Click download   button to download the result of DNA sequence to your computer.
2) Decode If you need to convert DNA sequence to a file one day, don't worry. The software provide the convenient and efficient method for you. Click choose  button on DECODE interface again. When you have selected a file, click submit   button. After that, you will watch NBA to wait for your file. The time of transformation may be long, so have a break. Then download your file.
